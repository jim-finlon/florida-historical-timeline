# Museum of Arts and Sciences: Daytona's Cultural Jewel
## 1971-Present

---

## Overview

The Museum of Arts and Sciences (MOAS) in Daytona Beach, opened in 1971, combines art, science, and history in one of Florida's most diverse cultural institutions. Situated on 90 acres of pristine Florida wilderness, the museum features an eclectic collection ranging from a 130,000-year-old giant ground sloth skeleton to Cuban art, Americana, African artifacts, and a complete 1950s-era Coca-Cola bottling plant. The museum's planetarium and nature trails make it a comprehensive educational destination that reflects the varied interests of the Volusia County community.
