# Kennedy Space Center Visitor Complex: Gateway to America's Space Program
## 1967-Present

---

## Overview

The Kennedy Space Center Visitor Complex on Merritt Island offers the public an immersive experience in the history, present, and future of American space exploration. What began in 1967 as a small facility to accommodate the growing public interest in the Apollo program has evolved into one of Florida's premier attractions, featuring authentic spacecraft, astronaut encounters, and behind-the-scenes access to NASA's active launch complex. From the Saturn V rocket that took humans to the Moon to the Space Shuttle Atlantis suspended in flight position, the Visitor Complex preserves the hardware and tells the stories of America's greatest technological achievements while inspiring future generations of explorers.
