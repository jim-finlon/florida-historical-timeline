# Florida Gators: The Swamp and Beyond
## 1906-Present

---

## Overview

The Florida Gators represent the University of Florida in Gainesville and compete in the Southeastern Conference, one of college athletics' most prestigious leagues. Since fielding their first football team in 1906, the Gators have grown into one of college sports' most successful all-around programs, winning national championships in football, basketball, baseball, and numerous Olympic sports. The program is perhaps best known for inventing Gatorade in 1965, the Swamp's deafening atmosphere, and producing legendary athletes including Emmitt Smith, Tim Tebow, and countless Olympic champions.
