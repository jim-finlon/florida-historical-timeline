# Boca Raton Museum of Art: Palm Beach County's Art Destination
## 1950-Present

---

## Overview

The Boca Raton Museum of Art, founded in 1950 as the Art Guild of Boca Raton, has evolved from a small community arts organization into a significant regional art museum with a collection of over 4,500 works. Located in the Mizner Park cultural complex since 2001, the museum showcases American and European art from the late 19th century to the present, alongside photography, sculpture, and works from Africa and Oceania. Its striking contemporary building has become an anchor of downtown Boca Raton's cultural renaissance.
