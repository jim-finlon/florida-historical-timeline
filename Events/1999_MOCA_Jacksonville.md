# Museum of Contemporary Art Jacksonville: Bold Art in the Bold New City
## 1999-Present

---

## Overview

The Museum of Contemporary Art Jacksonville (MOCA Jacksonville), located in the heart of downtown Jacksonville, is Northeast Florida's premier venue for modern and contemporary art. Established in 1999 as a partnership between the University of North Florida and the City of Jacksonville, MOCA occupies the historic Western Union Telegraph Building, a striking example of 1930s Art Deco architecture. The museum's collection of over 1,000 works focuses on art from 1960 to the present, with particular strengths in works on paper, video art, and photography.
