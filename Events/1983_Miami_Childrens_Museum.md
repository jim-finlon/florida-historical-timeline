# Miami Children's Museum: Learning Through Play
## 1983-Present

---

## Overview

The Miami Children's Museum, located on Watson Island between downtown Miami and Miami Beach, is one of the largest children's museums in the country, offering 56,500 square feet of interactive exhibits designed to encourage learning through play. Founded in 1983 and relocated to its striking waterfront facility in 2003, the museum serves Miami-Dade County's diverse population with bilingual programming and exhibits that celebrate the community's multicultural heritage. From a child-sized supermarket to a working television studio, the museum transforms everyday experiences into educational adventures for children and families.
