# Orlando Science Center: Central Florida's Science Discovery Hub
## 1955-Present

---

## Overview

The Orlando Science Center, located in the heart of downtown Orlando's Loch Haven Park cultural district, has inspired curiosity about science and technology in Central Florida visitors since 1955. What began as the Central Florida Museum evolved into a comprehensive science center featuring four floors of interactive exhibits, a digital planetarium, an observatory, and hands-on learning experiences. Serving as the region's primary informal science education institution, the Orlando Science Center connects the wonders of science to the everyday lives of the millions of residents and visitors in one of America's fastest-growing metropolitan areas.
