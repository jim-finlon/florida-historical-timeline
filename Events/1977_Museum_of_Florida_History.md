# Museum of Florida History: Telling Florida's Story
## 1977-Present

---

## Overview

The Museum of Florida History, located in Tallahassee's historic downtown adjacent to the State Capitol, is Florida's official state history museum. Opened in 1977 as part of the R.A. Gray Building complex, the museum tells the comprehensive story of Florida from prehistoric times through the present day. Through permanent and rotating exhibitions featuring artifacts, documents, and interactive experiences, the museum preserves and interprets the diverse cultures and events that have shaped the Sunshine State for more than 12,000 years of human habitation.
