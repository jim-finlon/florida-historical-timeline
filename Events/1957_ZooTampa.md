# ZooTampa at Lowry Park: Florida's Family Zoo
## 1957-Present

---

## Overview

ZooTampa at Lowry Park, consistently ranked among the top zoos in the nation, has served the Tampa Bay community since 1957. Located on 63 acres in North Tampa, the zoo is home to over 1,300 animals representing species from Florida and around the world, with special emphasis on native Florida wildlife, African animals, and Asian species. As a center for wildlife conservation and education, ZooTampa operates the region's primary manatee rehabilitation hospital and participates in numerous species survival programs, making it both a beloved family destination and a vital conservation institution.
