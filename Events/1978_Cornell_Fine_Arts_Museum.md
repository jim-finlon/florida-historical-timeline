# Cornell Fine Arts Museum: Winter Park's Academic Treasure
## 1978-Present

---

## Overview

The Cornell Fine Arts Museum at Rollins College in Winter Park is one of Florida's oldest and most distinguished college art museums. Founded in 1978, the museum houses a collection of more than 6,000 works spanning from antiquity to the present, with particular strengths in Old Master paintings, 19th-century American and European art, and contemporary works. Set on the picturesque Rollins College campus along Lake Virginia, the Cornell serves both the college community and the greater Central Florida region as a vital cultural resource.
