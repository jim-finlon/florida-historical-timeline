# Lowe Art Museum: South Florida's First Art Museum
## 1950-Present

---

## Overview

The Lowe Art Museum at the University of Miami, founded in 1950, holds the distinction of being South Florida's oldest art museum. Located on the university's Coral Gables campus, the museum houses a collection of over 19,000 works spanning 5,000 years of world art history, from ancient Egyptian and Greek antiquities to contemporary art. As an academic museum, the Lowe serves both the university community and the broader public, offering a scholarly approach to collecting and exhibiting that complements the region's other art institutions.
