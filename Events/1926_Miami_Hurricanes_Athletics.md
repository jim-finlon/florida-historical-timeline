# Miami Hurricanes: The U and the Championship Swagger
## 1926-Present

---

## Overview

The Miami Hurricanes, representing the University of Miami in Coral Gables, transformed college football in the 1980s with a brash, dominant style that produced five national championships and changed the sport forever. Known simply as "The U," Miami's football program combined South Florida swagger, NFL-caliber talent, and an intimidating mystique that made the orange and green among the most feared colors in sports. Beyond football, the Hurricanes have excelled in baseball, producing more Major Leaguers than any other school, while bringing big-time athletics to one of America's most dynamic metropolitan areas.
