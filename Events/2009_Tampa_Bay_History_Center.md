# Tampa Bay History Center: Gateway to Tampa Bay's Past
## 2009-Present

---

## Overview

The Tampa Bay History Center, opened in its current waterfront location in 2009, is the region's premier institution for the preservation and interpretation of Tampa Bay area history. Located along the Tampa Riverwalk in the city's revitalized downtown, the museum features 60,000 square feet of exhibition space covering 12,000 years of human presence in the Tampa Bay region. From the Tocobaga people through Spanish exploration, cigar-making traditions, and modern development, the center tells the complete story of one of Florida's most dynamic regions.
