# Professional Sports in Florida: From Dolphins to Lightning
## 1966-Present

---

## Overview

Florida has become one of America's premier professional sports markets, hosting teams in all major leagues and attracting millions of fans to stadiums from Jacksonville to Miami. Beginning with the Miami Dolphins in 1966, the state has grown to include nine major league franchises—the most of any state except California—across the NFL, MLB, NBA, NHL, and MLS. Florida's professional sports success includes multiple championships, legendary players, and an ever-growing fan base drawn from both longtime residents and transplants from across the nation who bring their sporting loyalties with them.
