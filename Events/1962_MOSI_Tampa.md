# MOSI: Tampa's Museum of Science & Industry
## 1962-Present

---

## Overview

The Museum of Science & Industry (MOSI), located in Tampa, is one of the largest science centers in the southeastern United States, offering hands-on exhibits, an IMAX dome theater, and educational programming that has inspired generations of young scientists since 1962. From its origins as the Tampa Museum of Science and Natural History, MOSI has evolved into a sprawling campus featuring a high-wire bicycle, Florida's only public planetarium, and immersive exhibits exploring everything from the human body to space exploration. The museum's mission to spark curiosity and innovation makes it a cornerstone of STEM education in the Tampa Bay region.
