# Samuel P. Harn Museum of Art: Florida's University Art Treasure
## 1990-Present

---

## Overview

The Samuel P. Harn Museum of Art at the University of Florida in Gainesville is one of the largest university art museums in the Southeast, housing a collection of more than 13,000 works spanning multiple cultures and 5,000 years of history. Opened in 1990 through a major gift from Samuel P. Harn, the museum has become a vital cultural resource for North Central Florida while serving as an essential teaching museum for university students. With particular strengths in Asian art, African art, photography, and modern and contemporary art, the Harn brings world-class exhibitions to a region far from Florida's coastal cultural centers.
