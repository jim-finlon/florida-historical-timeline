# Patricia & Phillip Frost Art Museum: FIU's Contemporary Vision
## 1977-Present

---

## Overview

The Patricia & Phillip Frost Art Museum at Florida International University is one of the largest academic art museums in the Southeast, featuring a collection of over 6,000 works with particular strengths in Latin American and Caribbean art. Founded in 1977 and relocated to its striking new facility in 2008, the museum serves as a vital cultural bridge between Miami's diverse communities and the international art world. Free to the public, the Frost Art Museum has become an essential component of Miami's vibrant arts landscape while fulfilling its academic mission as a teaching museum.
