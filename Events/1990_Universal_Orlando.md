# Universal Orlando Resort: The Movies Come to Life
## 1990-Present

---

## Overview

Universal Orlando Resort, opened in 1990, transformed Central Florida's tourism landscape by bringing Hollywood movie magic to the theme park experience. What began as a single park—Universal Studios Florida—has grown into a multi-park destination featuring Islands of Adventure, Volcano Bay water park, and the groundbreaking Wizarding World of Harry Potter. As Walt Disney World's primary competitor, Universal has pushed the boundaries of theme park technology and storytelling, creating some of the industry's most innovative attractions and forever changing visitor expectations.
